Chris Cornell

The man who saved a billion souls with his music

Here's a timeline of Chris Cornell's Life:

  • 1964 - Born in Seattle, WA.
  • 1984 - Formed Soundgarden with Kim Thayil and Hiro Yamamoto.
  • 1988 - Released the first album with Soundgarden named Ultramega OK.
  • 1989 - Release the second album, Louder Than Love, again with Soundgarden.
  • 1991 - Release the third Soundgarden album and formed the tribute band Temple of The Dog with members of the grunge band, Pearl Jam.
  • 2001 - Formed Audioslave with members of Rage Against The Machine.
  • 2010 - Returned his first band, Soundgarden.
  • 2017 - Last concert of Chris Cornell and death.
